About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1886, The Coca-Cola Company has grown to become one of the most recognizable and beloved brands in the world. With a commitment to refreshing the world and making a positive difference, our journey has been filled with innovation, inspiration, and impact.

From our humble beginnings in Atlanta, Georgia, we have expanded our presence to over 200 countries, offering a wide range of beverages that cater to diverse tastes and preferences. Our portfolio includes sparkling and still beverages, juices, dairy products, plant-based drinks, and more.

Our Mission

At Coca-Cola, our mission is simple: to refresh the world, inspire moments of optimism and happiness, create value, and make a difference. We strive to bring people together and make everyday moments more enjoyable with our refreshing beverages.
